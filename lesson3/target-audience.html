Target Audience

The target audience are those who are looking to be more active in the community, especially when it comes to helping out small businesses. It is those who want to particiapte or gain information about local events and fundraising. They will likely be acessing the site through their mobile phone, unless they are members of the committee -- then they will likely be using a computer. They

Persona 1

Person 1 is a small business owner who is actively part of the committee. She accesses the website on her computer most of the time when she sits down to concetrate on the material and her responsabilities. She is middle-aged and did not graduate college. Her business is now profitable, but she benefits from the fundraisers that the committe organizes to support small businesses like hers.

Persona 2

Person 2 is an older retired gentleman. He owned several sucessful large businesses which made him very rich. He recognizes that the economy thrives when small businesses are supported. He is not part of the committee, but he checks on the website from his mobile phone every week to see if there is any way he can donate money or time to help the community.

Scenarios

  1. Someone is looking for ways to participate and be active in the community
  2. A small business is looking for ways to grow their business
  3. Someone noticed there was a fun activity going on Main Street the other day and did some Googling to see what the fun event was about
  4. Someone was just curious what the "chamber of commerce" is.
