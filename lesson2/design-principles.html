Repitition

UPS Store

theupsstore.com

The UPS Store website has a repeating word theme. They wanted to demonstrate the services they offer by having an "ing" theme. They offer "printing", "shipping", "notorizing", and more "ing"s. This can been seen throughout their page.

Hicks Law

McDonalds

mcdonalds.com

The McDonalds website tries to offer simplicity. It follows Hick's law by attempting to not overwhelm the user with too many options. Notice how on the screen there are only two options. You can scroll down further to see another option, but the whole homepage only has a small handful of options or buttons to click on.

Alignment

Doordash

doordash.com

The Doordash home page does a great job at maintaining organization through alignment. Noticed how it displays a grid of the options to choose from. They are all neatly aligned with one another to make it easier for the users eyes to navigate.
