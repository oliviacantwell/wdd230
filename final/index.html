Welcome to Sandy Downs Equestrian Center

Our facility first started as a colt starting barn in 1990. When the original owners, Beth and Tuck Rogers, their daughter Tilly took over. Seeing how much joy horses brought to her own life and influenced her upbringing, she wanted to widden the barn's purpose to allow others to enjoy the equine atmosphere. Tilly and other riders at the facility have competed in the NCAA National Barrel Racing, the International Silver Cup Dressage Semi-finals, and Texas State Championship for Western Pleasure. Tilly strongly believes that all disciplines of riding have a place at the facility and encourages comradery and inter-disiplinary learning.

Sandy Downs offers boarding, leasing, colt starting, training/conditioning, and riding lessons. There are instructors who are trained in Dressage, Hunter, Barrel Racing, Western Pleasure, and Driving. Colt starting is provided in increments of 30 days (with a recommended minimum of 90 days). Training and conditioning is provided in 10 day increments. While we cannot assure the needed time to complete training, permission and communication will be provided before progressing to the next increment to gain owners approval. Driving lessons and training are not provided in the winter months. While we do not do sleigh ride training, boarders are welcome to still provide and use their own sleighs. Breeding for boarders is allowed but only upon special permission. We do not provide live cover or breeding stables, but there are foaling stalls available upon request.
