Target Audience

People who have an interest in pursueing equine related hobbies

Personas:

Carly: She does not own her own horse, but she wants to get started in learning about horses and how to ride.

Tim: Tim owns a horse and has just moved to the area. He is looking for the best option in the area for boarding his horse.

Scenarios

  1. Tim looks at the website and views the amenities at the facility to see if it matches what he is looking for.
  2. Tim views the boarding options and determines which boarding option would best fit for his budget and time.
  3. Carly looks at the lesson page to see if she would prefer to do group lessons or one on one lessons.
  4. Carly is not sure if she would be considered a beginner or an intermediate rider for the lesson program. She visits the "contact us" page to ask which program she would best match.
